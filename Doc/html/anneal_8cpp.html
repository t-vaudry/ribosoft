Annealing Temperature Score Used to calculate the annealing temperature of the ribozyme to the substrate. Using the MELTING library by Le Novère. MELTING, a free tool to compute the melting temperature of nucleic acid duplex. Bioinformatics, 17: 1226-1227.

Understanding return values:

  • R_INVALID_NUCLEOTIDE | sequence has an invalid nucleotide
  • R_STRUCT_LENGTH_DIFFER | sequence and structure lengths do not match
  • R_INVALID_CONCENTRATION | na_concentration or probe_concentration are out of range
Parameters
sequenceSubstrate sequence
structureSubstrate structure to determine binding regions
na_concentrationSodium (Na+) concentration (in moles)
probe_concentrationNucleic acid concentration in excess (in moles)
tempOut variable for annealing temperature score
